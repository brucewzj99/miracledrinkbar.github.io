Our Story

Miracle Drink Bar is a home grown independent passion driven business which aims to make cocktails affordable for everyone.

We specialize in making cocktails and mocktails. Having work as a bartender, we realize that the price of cocktails in the market is very high and we want to bring it down for the masses. Hence we come up with bottled cocktails, you get more, for less.
