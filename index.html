FAQ

  • What is the wait time for the drinks?
    Orders have to be made 4 days in advance.
  • How long can I keep the drinks for?
    3 days for optimal freshness (refrigerated) as I use fresh fruit juices!
  • How many can a bottle serve?
    Our bottle is good for 7-8 servings!
  • Do you have anything catered for those that do not drink alcohol
    Yes, you can check out our mocktail section!
  • Do you have a minimum spend?
    Yes, the minimum spend is $50. That is about 3 mocktails or 1 cocktail!
  • How much is the delivery fee?
    $15 per order if order is below $100
  • Is there free delivery?
    When you spend more than $100.
  • What time would the delivery arrive?
    Delivery time can be discussed!
  • Can you do same day delivery?
    No. Our products are handmade with fresh ingredients, hence we will need time prepare to serve you our best quality beverages.
  • I placed a wrong order, can I cancel?
    You must cancel two (2) days prior to the date of delivery.There will also be a cancellation fee of 10% of your total bill.
