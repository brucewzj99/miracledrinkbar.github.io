Our Story

Wanting something that can bring much more to every party and yet not break the bank, we come up with Miracle, a homegrown brand inspired from cocktails, we bring mocktails to you at affordable price!

What and why mocktail you may ask. We choose mocktail because it give you the feeling of sipping cocktail without the alcohol. Which means that anyone can drink and everyone can enjoy!

So what you waiting for? Zest up your party by bringing our mix there. Our drinks are only made on orders and are prepared by people just like you, we want the best for our bucks.
