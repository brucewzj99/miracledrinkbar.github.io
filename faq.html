FAQ

Here to answer your burning questions
How long can I keep the drinks?
3 days for optimal freshness (refrigerated) as I use fresh fruit juices!
How many can your bottle serve?
Our bottle is good for 7-8 servings!
Do you have anything catered for those that do not drink alcohol
Yes our drinks come in both alcoholic and non-alcoholic version!
Can I customise my label for my bottle?
It depends if time permits but I will try my best to accommodate!
Do you have a minimum spend?
Yes, the minimum spend is $50.
How much is delivery?
$15 per order if order is below $100
When do I get free delivery?
When you spend $100 with us.
What time would the delivery arrive?
Delivery time can be discussed and we will try to accommodate!
Can you do same day delivery?
No. Our products are handmade with fresh ingredients, hence we will need time prepare to serve you our best quality beverages.
I placed a wrong order, can I cancel?
You must cancel at two (2) days prior to the date of delivery else a there will be a cancellation fee of 10% of your total bill.
Where do you ship to?
Only within Singapore.
